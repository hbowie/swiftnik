elasticity

Elasticity refers to the way scrollable content seems to go past its bounds, slowing down gradually, and seeming to bounce, when the user has initiated scrolling and momentum is continuing the scroll. It should normally be on.

For further info:

  • https://developer.apple.com/documentation/appkit/nsscrollview/elasticity
