predominant axis scrolling

Some content is scrollable in both the horizontal and vertical axes, but is predominantly scrolled one axis at a time. Other content (such as a drawing canvas) should scroll freely in both axes. Traditionally this is not an issue with scroll wheels since they can only scroll in one direction at a time. With scroll balls and touch surfaces, it becomes more difficult to determine the user's intention. This property helps a scroll view determine the user's intention by specifying if there is a predominant scrolling axis for content.

For further info:
