Group

Groups don't have any effect on the interface as it appears to the user; instead, they just provide a work-around for the limitation of only allowing ten child views within a parent.

For something similar that will be visual to a user, use a Section instead.

For further info:

  • https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/group
